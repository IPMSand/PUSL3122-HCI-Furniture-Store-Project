## **2. Professional Summary (Expert Segment)**
*While Professionals represent 8.7% of users, they define the high-end technical requirements.*

* **Precision-First:** Unlike casual users, professionals require numerical sidebar inputs for 100% accuracy in client quotes.
* **High-Fidelity Rendering:** This group specifically prioritized realistic shading and textures to facilitate high-engagement sales.
* **Workflow Logic:** Preference for storage by project status (In Progress vs. Completed) to manage multiple client pipelines.
* **Efficiency Standards:** A critical need for keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Z) and desktop optimization.

## **3. Hobbyist & Homeowner Summary**
*The "Consumer" segment (91.3% of the data) focuses on visualization and ease of use.*

* **The "Visualization Gap":** The biggest fear is spatial fit—the worry that digital furniture won't fit real-world dimensions.
* **Ease of Entry:** Preference for pre-set templates and dragging wall segments rather than drawing from scratch.
* **Accessibility:** High demand for increased font sizes for measurements and high-contrast modes.
* **Communication:** Users want to explain their vision to others without needing technical CAD skills.
