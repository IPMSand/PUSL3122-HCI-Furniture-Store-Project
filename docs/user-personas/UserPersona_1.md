## Background

Thushara works at a mid-range furniture showroom, meeting 5–8 clients daily. She designs living rooms, dining rooms, bedrooms, office spaces, and business stores. Although she regularly uses digital tools, she is not a 3D software expert and prefers intuitive systems. She is often frustrated when 3D models do not accurately reflect lighting or real-world furniture placement.

---

## Goals

* Enter exact room dimensions quickly using numerical input
* Modify irregular wall shapes using drag templates
* Apply colors to individual furniture items
* Recover mistakes via Undo and confirmation pop-ups
* View a loading bar during model generation
* Work within a clean, clutter-free interface
* Export final designs as PDF or image

---

## Frustrations / Pain Points

* Slow loading times
* Cluttered interface with too many buttons
* Unrealistic lighting representation in 3D models
* Fear of losing work due to mistakes

---

## Key Non-Functional Needs

* Professional and minimal UI design
